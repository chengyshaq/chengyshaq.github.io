YuSheng Cheng
YuSheng Cheng is a professor at Anqing Normal University (AQNU), Anhui, China. He received his Ph.D. in the School of Computer and Information Science of Hefei University of Technology in 2007. His research interests concern the rough set theory and algorithm, semi supervised learning and data mining. He is the author of more than 50 papers in journals and conference proceedings such as Information Science, Knowledge-Based Systems, Neurocomputing, Applied Soft Computing, PAKDD and so on.
